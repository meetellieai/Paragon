What We Do

Complete Pest Protection

From common house pests to the toughest infestations, Paragon has the expertise and tools to get rid of them — and keep them gone.

🐜
General Pest Control
Comprehensive protection from ants, roaches, spiders, silverfish, earwigs, and all common household pests. Monthly, bi-monthly, or quarterly plans available.
🪲
Termite Control
Subterranean termite extermination and prevention with dry wood spot treatments and construction pre-treatments before they cause costly damage.
🦟
Mosquito Reduction
Take back your yard with our proven mosquito elimination programs. Protect your family from bites and the diseases mosquitoes carry.
🐀
Rodent Control
Full rodent exclusion and extermination — rats, mice, and other unwanted visitors. We find entry points and seal them for lasting results.
🦎
Wildlife Removal
Humane removal of nuisance animals including armadillos, snakes, and more. We believe in non-lethal methods and releasing animals back to the wild.
🐝
Bed Bug Treatment
Guaranteed bed bug elimination with our intensive treatment program. We'll have your home back to normal as quickly as possible.
